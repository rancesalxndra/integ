ALEXANDRA's BUCKET LIST



Back to Home Page





   When I reach the point in my life where I stop worrying about what others think of me, I will achieve all the things that I want. My bucket list consists of future achievements and mostly things that will gratify my future and child self.


  1.    Ride in a hot air balloon or attend at least five hot air balloon festivals across the globe. When I watched the movie 'Tangled' during my elementary school years and saw the scene focusing on hot air balloons, I instantly added it to my bucket list ever since then.

  2.    Travel to Korea and have a picture of certain locations of my favorite Kdrama scenes. I enjoy watching Kdrama so it would be an achievement to witness the locations in person.

  3.    Watch an NBA or Football game live, hopefully I get to watch my favorite Basketball and Football team.

  4.    Learn at least three foreign languages, specifically Italian, Spanish, and French. I am very much interested in learning new languages as it is an advantage when it comes to learning cultures of different countries and I think it would personally help me to improve my memory skills and socialization skills.

  5.    Read more than 10 books in a year. Every summer, when there is no school work to keep me busy I intend to read books to stimulate my mind and stay off of my phone. It keeps me sane and improves my vocabulary.

  6.    Donate and support charities of my choice every year. I believe that if you have been given a sufficient amount of funds, you should donate it to people in need.

  7.    Swim on a beach or in open water. Since I was a child, I was scared to swim even in a swimming pool. Now, I’ve conquered my fear to swim in a swimming pool yet on a beach or in open water.

  8.    Plant more trees and participate in nature activities. I like nature and I would do everything that I can to save it.

  9.    Build a freelance business and learn finance technology or anything about finance. It would mean so much to my future self if I started investing and become literate in finance technology.

  10.    Have a personal achievement of becoming independent. Sometimes, I tend to be dependent on my close friends and loved ones and I think it is not healthy so at most, I should try to live my life as my own and not become totally dependent on others.

RANCES, Christian Alexandra B.
