Smashed Chickpea Avocado Salad Sandwich is the perfect healthy lunch or dinner! This easy smashed chickpea salad can be eaten as a sandwich or served on greens, rice cakes, toast, or eaten as a dip with crackers or veggies!

Nutrition Value

Calories: 265kcal Carbohydrate: 35g
Protein: 11g Fat: 11g
Saturated Fat: 2g Vitamin A: 1374IU
Vitamin C: 25mg Calcium: 108mg

Ingredients

  • 14.5 ounces canned chickpeas drained and rinsed
  • 1 avocado ripe
  • ½ lemon juiced
  • ½ bunch cilantro chopped
  • 1 garlic clove minced
  • Kosher sea salt to taste
  • Kosher black pepper to taste
  • 2 celery stalks chopped
  • 1 tomato thinly sliced
  • 4 butter lettuce leaves
  • 4 slices whole wheat bread or whole grain

Instructions

  1.    Place half of the chickpeas in a food processor.

  2.    Add in the avocado, lemon, cilantro, garlic and season to your taste with salt and pepper.

  3.    Pulse several times until everything is minced, but not make it into a paste.

  4.    Transfer the mixture into a mixing bowl and stir in the remaining whole chickpeas and chopped celery.

  5.    Spread the filling over 2 of your bread slices. Top with sliced tomato and lettuce. Close the sandwiches with the remaining 2 slices of bread.
