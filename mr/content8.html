Enjoy! Dried fruit bits and honey add a touch of sweetness to this low-fat entree. As a bonus, the cottage cheese provides calcium.

Nutrition Value

Calories: 150 Total Fat: 2.5g Fiber: 33g
Protein: 12g Total Carbohydrates: 20g

Ingredients

  • 1 cup 2% milkfat low fat cottage cheese
  • 1/2 cup dried mixed fruit bits
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons maple syrup
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons chopped raw walnuts or pecans
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons lemon juice

Directions

  1.    Place the cottage cheese in a food processor and pulse until it is smooth.

  2.    Add the lemon juice and maple syrup and pulse until the mixture is well incorporated.

  3.    Set aside 1/2 the cottage cheese mixture for Saturday’s snack.

  4.    Place the remaining cottage cheese in a serving bowl and top with the strawberries, cinnamon, and chopped walnuts.
