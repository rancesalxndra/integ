This California Turkey Wrap recipe features a spinach wrap filled with delicious turkey, bacon, avocado, tomato, and lettuce. Makes easy, healthy, and satisfying lunch wraps that are tastier than store bought options. When you only have 15 minutes to eat, this quick and easy wrap is the way to go!

Nutrition Value

Calories: 342
15g fat (4g saturated fat)
37 mg cholesterol
1079 mg sodium
34g carbohydrate (5g sugars, 6g fiber)
18g protein

Ingredients

  • 2 whole wheat tortillas (8 inches), room temperature
  • 2 tablespoons fat-free mayonnaise
  • 1/4 pound thinly sliced deli turkey
  • 8 thin slices tomato
  • 2 teaspoons finely chopped jalapeno pepper
  • 1/4 cup shredded reduced-fat cheddar cheese
  • 2 teaspoons minced fresh cilantro
  • 1/2 medium ripe avocado, peeled and thinly sliced

Instructions

  1.    Spread tortillas with mayonnaise.

  2.    Top each with turkey, tomato, jalapeno, cheese, cilantro and avocado.

  3.    Roll up and cut in half.
