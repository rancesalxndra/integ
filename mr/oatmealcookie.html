Instructions

  1.    In a food processor, add oats, chia seeds, dates, peanut butter, salt, and honey.

  2.    Blend until a dough-like mixture is formed and no longer sticky.

  3.    Transfer the mixture into a bowl, fold in the chocolate chips, and use a spatula to mix until combined.
