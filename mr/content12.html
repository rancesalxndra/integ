When you're in a hurry yet need something hearty, this is the perfect breakfast or snack. It's especially useful during the summer, when we're all seeking for ways to cool off and spend less time in the kitchen.

Nutrition Value

Calories: 1709 Carbohydrates: 108g Protein: 57g
Fiber: 37g Sugar: 48g Fat: 130g

Ingredients

  • ½ cup strawberry (75 g)
  • ½ cup raspberry (60 g)
  • 1 banana, sliced
  • ½ cup greek yogurt (140 g)
  • ¼ cup almond milk (60 mL), or soy milk
  • ¼ cup peanut butter (60 g)

Directions

  1.    Add the berries, banana, Greek yogurt, almond milk, and peanut butter to a blender and blend until smooth.

  2.    Top with your favorite toppings.

  3.    Enjoy!
