When you're in a hurry yet need something hearty, this is the perfect breakfast or snack. It's especially useful during the summer, when we're all seeking for ways to cool off and spend less time in the kitchen.

Nutrition Value

Calories: 1709
Carbohydrates: 108g
Protein: 57g
Fiber: 37g
Sugar: 48g
Fat: 130g

Ingredients

  • ½ cup strawberry (75 g)
  • ½ cup raspberry (60 g)
  • 1 banana, sliced
  • ½ cup greek yogurt (140 g)
  • ¼ cup almond milk (60 mL), or soy milk
  • ¼ cup peanut butter (60 g)

Instructions

  1.    Add the berries, banana, Greek yogurt, almond milk, and peanut butter to a blender and blend until smooth.

  2.    Top with your favorite toppings.

  3.    Enjoy!

Recommended Exercises

SQUAT

A great exercise to tone the legs and the glutes and to strengthen the knee muscles.

BURPEES

An intense full body exercise that increases heart rate, strength, agility, and flexibility.

INCLINED DUMBBELL CURLS

Creates a deeper stretch on the biceps muscle tissue.

CABLE CURLS

The primary target of the cable curl is the biceps brachii muscle.
